My Favorite Concerts

I haven't been to a lot of concerts, but they have all been vastly different experiences. They all differ in location, quality, and size, but they all hold a special place in my heart for different reasons. These are my favorite concert experiences.

Some concerts i've been to

Beyoncé

There's not much to say about this one. It's beyoncé She's literally a goddess... obviously it was good.

Echosmith

This was a great concert because I planned it at my school, but unfortunatley I couldn't go. I was a part of my CAB (Camput activities board) and we planned this over the whole semester. Being in CAB, i was chosen to go to a convention in Kentucky. So, while i was away, everyone on camput was able to enjoy the fruits of my labor. I'm not salty or anything though...

Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is one of my favorite artists, periodt. Her voice is unmatched, and she saved pop music tbh. The thing that made this concert special, however, is the fact that I was 4 rows away from the stage. I'd never been this close to the stae before and it was really special for me.
